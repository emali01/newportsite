Dolomite Granules (0.7mm - 4mm)

Premium quality dolomite granules for various industrial and construction applications

Product Description

Our dolomite granules are carefully processed and graded to sizes ranging from 0.7mm to 4mm, ensuring consistent quality and performance. These granules are derived from high-purity dolomite stone, offering excellent physical and chemical properties for a wide range of applications.

Key Properties

  • High whiteness and brightness
  • Excellent thermal stability
  • Low moisture absorption
  • Chemical inertness
  • High compressive strength
  • Weather resistance

Specifications

Parameter Specification
Particle Size 0.7mm - 4mm (custom sizes available)
CaO Content 30-32%
MgO Content 20-22%
SiO2 Content < 1%
Whiteness 90-95%
Moisture Content < 0.5%
Bulk Density 1.5-1.7 g/cm³

Applications

Construction

Used in decorative concrete, terrazzo flooring, and as filler in construction materials

Manufacturing

Raw material for glass, ceramics, and steel production

Agriculture

Soil conditioner to neutralize acidic soils and provide magnesium

Texture Wall Plaster

Premium decorative plaster for creating beautiful textured wall finishes

Product Description

Our texture wall plaster is a high-quality, ready-to-use decorative coating that creates beautiful textured finishes on interior and exterior walls. Formulated with premium dolomite and other natural minerals, it provides excellent adhesion, durability, and weather resistance.

Key Properties

  • Excellent workability and spreadability
  • Crack-resistant formulation
  • Water-resistant and breathable
  • UV stable colors
  • Low VOC content
  • Mold and mildew resistant

Specifications

Parameter Specification
Base Material Dolomite-based with polymer additives
Coverage 1-1.5 kg/m² per mm thickness
Drying Time 24-48 hours (depending on conditions)

Shelf Life 12 months in original packaging
